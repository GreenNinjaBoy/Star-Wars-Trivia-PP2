Star-Wars Trivia

Test Your Star-Wars Knowledge with this fun Online Quiz!

Game Rules

This Game is based on the 9 main feature films (episodes I - IX)

  • Each quiz is made is made up of 20 random questions
  • The quiz has three different difficulties Padawan (easy), Jedi Knight (medium), or Jedi Master (Hard).
  • You will have 15 seconds to answer each question.
  • Failure to answer within that time results in a automatic incorrect answer.
  • To select an answer, simply click or tap (if on touch screen device) on the one you think is correct.
  • Try not to cheat as this is ment to be fun.
